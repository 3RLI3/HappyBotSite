How to Use HappyBot

  1. Add on Telegram: Open t.me/HappySingaporeBot and tap “Start.”
  2. Say Hello: Type /start or just send “Hi.”
  3. Explore Commands: Type /help to see all features.
  4. Tip: Send a voice note—HappyBot will transcribe and reply with text & voice!
